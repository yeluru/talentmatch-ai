# What's New - February 12, 2026

Hi team! Here's what we improved this week to make your job easier.

---

## For Recruiters: Better Candidate Search

**What changed:**
- You now get 50-200 candidate results instead of just 10
- Paste any job description and it automatically finds the skills
- Click skills to turn them on or off before searching
- Your searches are saved so you can come back to them later

**How to use it:**
1. Go to Talent Search
2. Paste a job description in the top box
3. Click on skills to select what you want
4. Hit Search - you'll see way more candidates

---

## For Account Managers: No More Lost Work

**What changed:**
- When adding a client, your typed info stays saved even if you switch tabs
- You can add a second contact person if needed

**How to use it:**
1. Click "Add Client"
2. Start typing - your info saves automatically
3. Switch tabs to look up information - it'll be there when you get back
4. Add a second contact if the client has two people you work with
